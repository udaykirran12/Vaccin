About:

Welcome to our webpage dedicated to COVID-19 vaccination in India.
The COVID-19 pandemic has impacted the world, and India has been one
of the countries significantly affected by the virus. As the second
most populous country in the world, India faced unique challenges in
managing the spread of the virus. However, through a robust vaccination
campaign and collective efforts, India is striving towards overcoming
this crisis and safeguarding its population.

Vaccination Steps:

1. Login/Signup with your details.

2. Book Your Slot with the Nearest center.

3. Visit your Center on the booking day.

4. Get your vaccination certificate.
